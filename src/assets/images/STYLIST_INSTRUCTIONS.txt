INSTRUCTIONS FOR STYLISTS - ADDING NEW PHOTOS

To add new portfolio photos to the website:

1. Open the "gallery" folder
2. Add your new photos to this folder
3. Name them image10.jpg, image11.jpg, etc. (continuing the sequence)
4. Make sure your images are JPG or PNG format
5. Keep file sizes under 2MB each

That's it! Your new photos will automatically appear on the website.

For hero images or founder photos:
- Use the "hero" folder for banner images
- Use the "about" folder for founder/team photos

Questions? Contact the web team for assistance.
